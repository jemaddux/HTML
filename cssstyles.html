How CSS3 can style your website pages

Intro

CSS3 is the lastest generation Cascading Style Sheet and what it does is to provide the consistent look that you want to your webpages across various browsers. It can do many things from creating the border you see around this whole section to changing how your text and pictures look. This text you are reading right now is in the "content" section and using CSS3 I have styled it to give it that cool border with the rounded edges and to auto align it in the center of the page. It even changes size with the size of your browser window. Go ahead and resize your browser window to see how this section will continue to stay at 70% the size of the window. This allows this page to work not only in full size browsers but on mobile devices too.
